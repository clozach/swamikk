Design explorations · sales page

Five ways to sell one course.

Five self-contained sales-page concepts for the 2026 Developing Resilience Online Course with Swami Karma Karuna — each a distinct world built from the same fixed, verbatim course copy, on the Anahata design system.

Every page carries the identical body copy; only the selection, sequencing, typesetting and pacing differ. Open each below.

01

The long-form letter (VSL)

A true video-sales-letter arc in prose: agitate with the three questions, breathe through refrain bands over the mountains, reveal the toolkit, and return four times to a spec-worded call to enrol.

StandoutThe four-part CTA-chorus rhythm — each Enrol button introduced by a different verbatim spec sentence — is the best repeated, spec-faithful path-to-purchase in the whole set.

02

The Bihar yantra

Sacred geometry as a layout system: a twelve-petal Anahata lotus and shatkona drawn once and reused as section marks, roman-numeral “movements,” a bead-on-thread mala, and a lotus that fills as you scroll.

StandoutThe reusable yantra geometry plus the literal “mala” — six mantras as beads strung on a rust thread — sacred geometry deployed as an actual layout system, not sprinkled decoration.

View exploration →
03

The editorial magazine

A print feature: masthead and dateline, a cover with Fig. 1, justified two-column body with an At-a-Glance sidebar, full-bleed figure bands, a dot-leadered Contents index, and a tipped-in bind-in reply card.

StandoutThe “Contents” index — a true magazine table-of-contents with dotted leaders and run-times — is the single most elegant treatment of the what’s-included data anywhere in the set, and it sidesteps card-clutter entirely.

View exploration →
04

The breath-paced page

The page is a breath cycle: cream “inhale” text beats alternate with full-bleed “exhale” photo beats, threaded by a persistent mala plumb-line and revealed by pure-CSS scroll timing.

StandoutThe inhale/exhale structural rhythm driven by CSS animation-timeline: view(): motion that literally IS the breath concept, needs no JS, and degrades to fully-visible static content where unsupported or when reduced-motion is set — the reference implementation of the brief’s motion clause.

View exploration →
05

The handwritten letter

An intimate first-person letter: Caveat handwriting for the asides over a Playfair body, tipped-in tilted “snap” photos, an enclosure of what is sent, and an RSVP reply card to close.

StandoutThe epistolary conceit carried end-to-end — the pull-quote set as a margin-ruled confidence, spec phrases (“return to your center,” “rise from challenges”) reused as handwritten photo captions, and the signature sign-off — the warmest, most human read in the set.
